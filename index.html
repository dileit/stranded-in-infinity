This is a guessing game using quotes from characters played in a tabletop game, Stranded In Infinity. When you click 'Start!', the game will generate a quote at random. You then have to select the character at the left and click 'Check!' to confirm your answer. It will display the character name, image and if you were correct in your selection. There are 5 questions per game in total. Please click 'NEXT' to move on to a new question, 'SKIP' to avoid the question, and 'RESET' to reload the game.
